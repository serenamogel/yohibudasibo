    Mini Pineapple Cheesecake Trifle

    These Mini Pineapple Cheesecake Trifles are loaded with pineapple flavor! Perfect for an after-school snack, dessert, or party! Made with just 4 ingredients, this easy pineapple trifle recipe can be prepared as individual servings or in a traditional trifle dish.

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword mini pineapple trifle, trifle recipe Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 6 Calories 99kcal Author Trish ‚Äì Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 ounces cream cheese or light cream cheese
    • 20 ounces pineapple chunks I used Dole
    • 4 ounces whipped topping or 1/2 cup heavy cream, whipped
    • 1 cup crushed vanilla wafers

    Instructions

    • Strain the pineapple juice and set aside.

    • Quarter pineapple chunks and set aside.

    • In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese until light and fluffy.

    • Beat in 2 tablespoons of reserved pineapple juice and 1/2 cup pineapple chunks.

    • Fold in whipped topping or whipped cream.

    • In small jars or dishes, layer one tablespoon of crushed vanilla wafers, one to two tablespoons of pineapple cheesecake, one to two tablespoons of pineapple chunks and repeat until the jar or dish is filled. Top with a mini vanilla wafer and a few pieces of pineapple.

    Notes

    If using whipped cream, you may want to sweeten the cheesecake mixture with a bit of powdered sugar.
