    Jordynne Grace Shares What She Is Most Thankful For

    Jordynne Grace is living the dream. WrestleZone recently spoke with TNA/IMPACT Wrestling star Jordynne Grace, who will challenge Trinity for the Knockouts Championship at the Hard To Kill pay-per-view.

    17 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Jordynne Grace is living the dream.

    WrestleZone recently spoke with TNA/IMPACT Wrestling star Jordynne Grace, who will challenge Trinity for the Knockouts Championship at the Hard To Kill pay-per-view.

    Thanksgiving is here in the United States. In the spirit of the national holiday, we asked the former Knockouts Champion what she’s grateful for.

    “Man, I’m thankful for my five dogs, my two cats, my incredible husband, my family, and being able to live my dream every day since I was 14 years old,” Grace said.

    The TNA Hard To Kill pay-per-view will take place on Saturday, January 13, 2024 at the Palms Casino and Resort in Las Vegas.

    In addition to the Knockouts Championship match between Jordynne Grace and Trinity, one other match has been confirmed for the card so far. Alex Shelley will defend his TNA World Championship against Moose, who is cashing in his Feast or Fired briefcase.

    Stay tuned for our full interview with Jordynne Grace soon on WrestleZone.
