    Michelle McCool Training Her Daughter in the Sport of Boxing

    09 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Michelle McCool has been regarded as one of the most enthralling pioneers of the wrestling business. While she has been out of the ring for years and only made sporadic appearances, it seems like she is introducing her daughter to the combat world.

    The former WWE Divas champion posted series of Instagram stories, in the gym with her daughter, Kaia Faith Calaway. The mother-daughter duo can be spotted training together, with Michelle teaching her young one some boxing punches. Later on, Kaia can be seen landing continuous punches to a gym bag.

    The young Calaway’s strength was on full display as McCool mentioned that he wrists were bruised from defending punches from Kaia.

    “Love training with my mini. My wrists are also bruised from defending her punches! #hithard.”

    Kaia is the daughter of Michelle McCool and the legendary WWE Hall of Famer, The Undertaker. She has been often spotted on WWE shows with her parents, and has witnessed both of them compete inside the squared circle.

    With her already indulging in combat sports, it will be interesting to see if Kaia would follow in the footsteps of her iconic parents and pursue a career in this world.
