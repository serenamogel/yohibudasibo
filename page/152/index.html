Can my signature be anything?

27 Jan 2024, 00:00

The signature can be made by anything that marks the paper. Pencil is not favored because it can smudge and be erased, but a signature made with a pencil is equally valid as a signature in pen. Signatures can also be made with stamps or with electronic means, since these are all different forms of writing implements. Can my signature be a symbol? Legally, in the US, any mark you make signifying intent is allowed to be your signature.
