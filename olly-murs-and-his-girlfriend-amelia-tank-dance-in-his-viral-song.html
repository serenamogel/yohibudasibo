    Olly Murs And His Girlfriend Amelia Tank Dance In His Viral Song

    13 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Olly Murs and his fiancée Amelia Tank danced to the English singer-song songwriter’s “Dance With Me Tonight,” which was published in 2011. On December 2, 2021, the famous musician turned to TikTok to show off his dancing routines with his lover after the song got popular on the short-video sharing app.

    Olly Murs and Amelia Tank’s Dance

    In the video, the two lovebirds can be seen leaping and dancing to Murs’ song in numerous locations around the United Kingdom. They began by dancing in front of the red telephone box located just under Big Ben in London. They were observed in the video’s later transactions at Buckingham Palace, The London Eye, and Westminster Abbey.

    Murs donned a black sweatshirt and grunge jeans with white sneakers, while his girlfriend wore a grey hoodie and black denim with black Converse shoes. She also wore a similar black beanie hat, allowing her blonde hair to fall about her shoulders.

    How Did the Song Go Viral?

    The song became popular when two closest friends and TikTok stars, Em and Loz, posted a video on November 8, 2021, showcasing their dancing talents. After Murs shared his dancing video on the app, Em and Loz’s official TikTok account [@EmandLoz] also reacted to the singer’s video.

    “Our dancing + your music Equals vibrations,” the remark said. In response to the remark, he complimented the other producers for reintroducing his decade-old music to the market. He wrote, “Massively! You’ve taken a song I wrote ten years ago and brought it into the year 2021! I’m very amazed by all of the videos! Insane! Tour tickets for next year?” Murs had earlier applauded Em and Loz by commenting on their initial video. In terms of the music, ‘Dance With Me Tonight is a single by Murs from his second studio album, In Case You Didn’t Know. The song’s YouTube video, which was posted in October 2011, has had over 112 million views as of this writing.

    Olly Murs and Amelia Tank’s Relationship

    Murs stated in December 2019 that he was dating Tank, a fitness fanatic and bodybuilding champion. In an interview with The Sun in July 2021, he stated Tank was the person he wanted to marry and spend the rest of his life with.
    He stated that he had promised to marry his fiancée, stating, “If I mess this up, I’ll seem like a moron since it’s everything I want. Amelia is the one I’ll be with for the rest of my life.”

    The musician also confessed that the pair had reached a stage in their life where they were ready to take the next step and have a family. The tank also spoke out during the meeting, echoing her boyfriend’s comments. She said that she loved her spouse and could not see herself with anybody else. Aside from being lovers, the two are gym partners and strong supporters of one other’s jobs. While the singer has gained the greatest physique with the help of his girlfriend, she won a bodybuilding competition in 2021.
