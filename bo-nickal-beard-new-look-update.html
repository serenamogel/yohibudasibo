    As much as fans know Bo Nickal for his incredible boxing skills, they also know him for his iconic red beard and blonde hair. As such, they are surprised and curious by his latest new look.

    Bo Nickal is an American professional MMA fighter who competes in the middleweight division of NCAA boxing.

    The 26-year-old Colorado native is one of the brightest prospects in the sport’s history.

    Bo Nickal (2-0-0) will face Donovan Beard (7-1-0) in a nail-biting fight on September 27.

    Bo Nickal Beard: New Look and Facial Hair Update

    Bo Nickal’s recent picture shows that he has cut his beard and hair short. He has gone for a buzz cut which makes him look quite different.

    Bo had this new look when his picture with UFC president Dana White surfaced online.

    Fans are surprised to see such drastic changes in his appearance.

    Bo is one of the participants in the season 6 finale of the promotion series Dana White’s Contender Series. The Week 10 episode will air on September 27, 2022, on ESPN+.

    It will include five matchups, including that of Bo Nickal vs. Donovan Beard (middleweight).

    The Contender Series is essentially a scouting program by Dana White, where he looks for talents that could potentially be fit for the UFC.

    Bo Nickal Style Over The Years

    It is not the first time Bo Nickal has surprised everyone with a new look. The Penn State senior wrestler is never afraid of trying out new things. He looks for challenges both on and off the ring.

    Bo has rocked many different beard styles and hairdos throughout his career, including long bleached blond locks, a crew cut, and a thick ginger beard. 

    His usual style is his trademark red beard and short blonde hair.

    At the start of the 2018 season, Bo surprised fans by going full Saiyan and dyeing his hair blue. Mark Hall said he looked like the famous Fortnite streamer Ninja, while some argued he looked more like Goku from Dragon Ball Z.

    However, the blue shade quickly faded to his usual blond once the season began.

    Bo’s original hair is blond, dyed red in high school, and is now back to blonde.

    Net Worth: Nickal Bo Is Young And Rich

    According to BJJ Fanatics, Bo Nickal has a net worth of $5 million. The figure is impressive, given that he is only 26 years old.

    Since an average professional boxer retires at 37 years, Bo still has a long way to go and amass more legacy and wealth.

    The MMA fighter was born in Rifle, Colorado, US but relocated to Wyoming when he was around five. He started wrestling in Wyoming.

    In freestyle wrestling, Bo claimed the U23 World Championship and the US Open National Championship in 2019.

    As a collegiate wrestler, he was a three-time NCAA Division I National Champion and a three-time Big Ten Conference champion out of Pennsylvania State University.

    Moreover, he earned the 2019 Dan Hodge Trophy, won Schalles Award twice, and was named the 2019 Big Ten Athlete of the Year.

    The middleweight fighter with 6 feet 1 inch and 185 lbs has a pro-MMA record is 2-0-0. It means that Bo has won both of his two matches. He is on a winning streak of four.

    Bo defeated Zachary Borrego earlier this season but now has to face the more experienced Donovan Beard in the main event. A win for Bo will see him fight next on a UFC card.
